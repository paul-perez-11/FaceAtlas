## 🎯 Overview

**FaceAtlas** is a fully responsive skincare e-commerce platform developed as the final project for **ITS121-1L — Web Systems and Technologies 1 Laboratory** at Mapúa University.

The platform combines a curated skincare product marketplace with science-backed educational content, featuring an innovative **interactive facial image map** that allows users to explore common skin diseases and concerns by clicking on specific facial areas.

>>> *"Mapping faces, unlocking skincare knowledge — one click at a time."*

---

## Vision
To become a one-stop e-commerce platform that retails premium skincare products while providing science-backed information on skin types and concerns through ingredient transparency and authentic user feedback.

## Mission
To empower people with confidence in their own skin by helping them make informed decisions tailored to their individual skincare needs.

---

#### ✨ Features
**🗺️ Interactive Image Mapping**
* **Facial Navigation System:** Clickable face diagram where users can explore skin concerns by specific facial zones (forehead, cheeks, chin, nose, etc.)
* **Zone-Specific Information:** Each mapped area reveals common skin conditions, recommended products, and care tips
* **Visual Learning:** Intuitive, visual approach to understanding skin anatomy and concerns
#### 🛒 E-Commerce Functionality
* **Product Catalog:** Browse skincare products with detailed descriptions
* **Ingredient Transparency:** Full ingredient lists with explanations of benefits
* **Searchable Database:** Find products and ingredients quickly
Subscription Service: Regular delivery options for skincare essentials
#### 🧪 Skin Type Guide
* **Personalized Recommendations:** Input forms to determine individual skin types
* **Tailored Product Suggestions:** Products matched to specific skin concerns
* **Educational Content:** Science-backed articles on skincare routines
#### 📱 Responsive Design
* **Mobile-First Approach:** Optimized for all devices (mobile, tablet, desktop)
* **CSS Media Queries:** Adaptive layouts, font sizes, and animations
* **Accessibility:** Semantic HTML5 for screen readers and assistive technologies
#### 🎨 Aesthetic Design
* **Pink & Cozy Theme:** Feminine, calming color palette targeting the primary audience
* **Clean Typography:** Easy-to-read fonts optimized for wellness content
* **Smooth Animations:** CSS transitions for engaging user experience

## 💻 Usage

#### **Exploring the Face Map**
1. Navigate to the Skin Guide page
2. Hover over different facial zones on the interactive face diagram
3. Click on a specific area (forehead, cheeks, chin, etc.)
4. View detailed information about common skin concerns in that zone
5. Browse recommended products for those specific concerns

#### **Shopping Experience**
1. Browse products on the Products page
2. Use filters to sort by skin type, concern, or ingredient
3. Click on products for detailed ingredient lists
4. Add items to cart and proceed to checkout

#### Finding Your Skin Type
1. Visit the Skin Guide section
2. Complete the skin type assessment form
3. Receive personalized product recommendations
